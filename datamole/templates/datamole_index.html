About us

智能感知与数据研究室成立于2012年，主要研究传感器数据的主动感知，研究方向为信息融合、状态估计，涉及应用领域包括室内定位与跟踪、机器人运动行为理解、智能环保决策、时序信号分析等。基于这些研究领域，研究室目前承担国家自然科学基金等多项研究项目，发表EI、SCI检索、核心期刊论文50余篇，拥有多项授权国家发明专利及软件著作权。

About us

As researchers focus on multisensor fusion, we had deeply studied the theory and methods of multi-target tracking based on Baysian estimation, such as Kalman filter, etc. Nowadays, we found that we have to deal with more and more sequence data. Some of them are from sensors, some of them from networks, and some of them even from ourselves！What a big data! Here we focus on the time-series data with enormous amount, which is exist in many systems, such as Cyber-Physical Systems(CPS) and Internet of things(IoT), etc. Our aim is to cognitive the trends of the enormous data, and to predict the future.
